A bit about me.

I am...

01

an enthusiastic frontend developer

During my studies, I was forced to quickly learn the basics of React for a project from one of the courses, which I managed to achieve in less than a week. This is how my adventure with JS-based frameworks for creating web applications began.

Soon after, in the summer of 2021, I took on my first job, where this time I had to quickly assimilate the basics of programming in Vue.js, which I also accomplished at a swift pace and despite a few setbacks in the first weeks of work, after a few months I became one of the most valuable employees of the company.

After less than a year, I received a job offer for a European Union project at the University of Warsaw, which I of course accepted. At UW, I continued to polish my proficiency in Vue.js as well as pure JS, HTML, CSS and even in the field of UI/UX design, as I was responsible for both designing and implementing the user interface.

02

a graduate of the best Polish technical university

In February 2022, I obtained a bachelor's degree in computer science from the Faculty of Electronics and Information Technology at the Warsaw University of Technology, where I am currently continuing my studies at the master's level.

03

a hobbyist digital artist

I started my journey with digital art sometime in 2017. Since then I've learned Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator - generally mostly 2D though recently I've been playing more and more with 3D using Blender.

As for now, my forte is creating natural landscapes but in the future I'd like to expand my skills with drawing characters, machines, architecture and other fun stuff! You can check some of my artworks in the section below.
